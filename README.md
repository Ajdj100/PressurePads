# PressurePads

Enables independently managing tactical devices. Toggle and switch modes on your flashlights, lasers, and illuminators with ease. No more switching between flashlight and laser. Turn them both on and off at will.

---

## Features

* Toggle tactical devices independently with two customizable hotkeys (light/laser)
* Switch modes on devices separately with `modifier + hotkey`

### Control Modes
**Standard Mode**
* Click hotkey to toggle device

**Advanced Mode**
* Hold key for momentary activation
* Double-tap to toggle device
